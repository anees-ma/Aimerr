Search Engine Optimisation

Search Engine Optimisation(SEO) is the process of improving website ranking in search engine results, locally as well as globally and thus promoting your business. It improves the scope and visibility of your website in the online and there by increasing your scope of business. SEO is one of the best online advertising techniques for getting online presence thus boosting your business. It enhances your website’s presence and helps to get higher rank in Google's organic search results or search engine's result pages(SERPs) and is a continues process and demands regular monitoring and analysis of the performance of your website. SEO can increase your page clicks, views and traffic to your website. Social Media Optimisation(SMO) is also a part of SEO and is effectively improving the reflection of your brand or business on social networking sites like facebook, twitter, google plus, instagram, pinterest etc. People from all over the world depend on social media to draw insights, build their network thus promoting their brand or business.


The tactics we used for Search Engine Optimisation are keyword research, onpage optimisation, offpage optimisation, link building and social media optimisation.

Tactics we use

Keyword research is one of the most important, valuable, and high return activities in seo. Ranking for the right keywords can make or break your website. By researching your market's keyword demand, you can not only learn which terms and phrases to target with SEO, but also learn more about your customers as a whole.

On-page SEO is the practice of optimizing individual web pages in order to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. On-page refers to both the content and HTML source code of a page that can be optimized, as opposed to off-page SEO which refers to links and other external signals.

Offpage SEOis the actions taken outside of your own website to impact your rankings within search engine results pages (SERPs). Optimizing for off-site ranking factors involves improving search engine and user perception of a site's popularity, relevance, trustworthiness, and authority.

Link building is the practice of reaching out and trying to individually bring links to specific URLs or specific domains — usually individual pages, though — and trying to use those links to boost the rankings of those pages in search engine results.

Social media optimization (SMO) is the process of increasing the awareness of a product, brand or event by using a number of social media outlets and communities to generate viral publicity.
